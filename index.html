About

I am a fifth-year graduate student in the Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences Program at Princeton University whose interests lie at the intersection of tropical atmospheric dynamics, idealized general circulation models, and Python. In the past I have worked on simulating the climate impacts of aerosols released from forest fires and investigated the varied future projections of lightning flash density by different lightning parameterizations.
